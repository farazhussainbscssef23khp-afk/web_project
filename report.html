Minimum 10 characters required
You can type the address or use auto-detection

Reporting Guidelines

What to Report:

  • Broken streetlights or traffic signals
  • Potholes or damaged roads
  • Garbage overflow or illegal dumping
  • Damaged sidewalks or pedestrian crossings
  • Blocked drainage or water issues
  • Damaged public property or facilities

Reporting Tips:

  • Be specific and detailed in your description
  • Include the exact location or nearby landmarks
  • Take clear photos when possible
  • Report issues as soon as you notice them
  • One issue per report for better tracking
